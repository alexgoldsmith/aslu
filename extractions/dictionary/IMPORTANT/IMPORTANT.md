In the sign for "important" your hands start in front of your belly and arc up and out to the sides then 
  come together in front of your chest:IMPORTANT::The handshape is a sideways (palm-in) "F"-handIMPORTANT:Animation:  IMPORTANTNote:The sign for "important" can also mean "worthy."Note:If you omit the first part of the sign (the big arcing movement) and 
			just do the very last part of the sign (where the "F" hands come 
			together) using a double movement this sign can mean "worth" (as in 
			"the cost of or value of something."You can learn AmericanSign
